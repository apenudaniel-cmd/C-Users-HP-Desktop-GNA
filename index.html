Our Mission

GNA Healthcare's mission is to bridge the gap between hospital care and home-based recovery by providing a professional, technology-enabled, and compassionate nursing service right to your doorstep. We are committed to fostering wellness and independence in the community.

Compassion

Treating every client with dignity, empathy, and respect.

Professionalism

Delivering the highest standard of non-emergency care.

Accessibility

Making quality healthcare available in home and community settings.

Our Professional Services

Post-Operative Care

Skilled nursing services to manage wounds, medication, and recovery after hospital discharge, ensuring a smooth transition home.

  • Wound dressing and monitoring
  • Pain management support
  • Patient and family education

Chronic Disease Management

Assistance for patients managing long-term conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and asthma, with routine monitoring and education.

  • Vital signs checks (BP, Glucose)
  • Medication administration/reminders
  • Diet and lifestyle counseling

General Wellness Checks

Regular check-ups and preventative care tailored for the elderly, children, or anyone needing non-emergency, in-home care.

  • Immunizations and injections
  • Health risk assessments
  • Tele-consultation support
